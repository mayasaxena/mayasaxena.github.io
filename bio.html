Biography

Maya Saxena was born in St. Paul, MN on June 9th, 1994. Six months later, her family moved to Austin, TX, where she was raised. She grew up in a home full of family members including her younger sister, parents, and various grandparents, aunts and uncles. She attended St. Stephen's Episcopal School from 2005 to 2012, when she graduated with honors. During her time at St. Stephen's she was active in many extracurricular activities including theatre and choir. She discovered her interest in computer science through a summer program at Columbia University, and proceeded to take every CS course her high school offered.

She began attending Tufts University in the School of Engineering in the fall of 2012 as a computer science major. This is when she joined the Tufts Tae Kwon Do team and found her passion for martial arts. Minoring in Multimedia Arts allowed her to pursue her lifelong love of the arts in combination with computer science and engineering.
